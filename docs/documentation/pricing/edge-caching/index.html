On Demand Pricing

You will be charged based on product usage by the sum of the values calculated for the Total Data Transferred, Requests, and Real-Time Purge Operations metrics.

Total Data Transferred

Refers to the total volume of GB transferred by Azion for all customer domains, regardless of the modules enabled in each edge application. It is the sum of (a) traffic between users and Azion, and (b) traffic between Azion and origin, even when the origin is an Azion service, such as Live Ingest, L2 Caching, among others.

Real-Time Purge Operations

This is the number of operations to purge objects from the cache, whether by URLs, Wildcard URLs, or Cache Keys.

The first 1,000 purge operations each month are included for all customers. From this volume on, you will pay $ 5 per 1,000 additional purges.

Pricing Example

If your applications received 750 million requests from the United States in 1 month, consuming total traffic of 30 TB, and you were within the limit of 1,000 purges that month, the amount will be calculated as follows:

Total Data Transferred

The first 10 TB of traffic is priced at $ 0.085 per GB, so 10,000 x $ 0.085 = $ 850.

The next 20 TB of traffic is priced at $ 0.080 per GB, so 20,000 x $ 0.080 = $ 1,600.

In this example, the value for the month will be $ 2,450.

Requests

Requests are billed every 10,000 for the unit price of $ 0.009, so (750,000,000 / 10,000) x $ 0.009 = $ 675.

Total charges

The total amount to be paid in the month will be $ 3,125, according to the simulation.

L2 Caching
