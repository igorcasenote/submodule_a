On Demand Pricing

You will be charged based on your use of Azion products calculated by the Support Services Fee metric.

Support Services Fee

Calculated as (a) a specified minimum monthly amount or, if higher than this minimum value, (b) a percentage of your monthly usage costs for all other Azion products and services during the billing period.

Pricing Example

If your monthly consumption with Azion in one month was $ 90,000, the support amount will be calculated as follows:

Enterprise:

For the first 10,000 USD, 10% will be charged, thus 1,000 USD support.
For the next 70,000 USD, 7% will be charged, therefore 4,900 USD support.
For the remaining $ 10,000, 5% will be charged, so $ 500 support.

In this simulation, the total amount of Support Enterprise will be $ 6,400 in the month.

Mission Critical:

For the first 60,000 USD, 10% will be charged, thus 6,000 USD support.
For the remaining 30,000 USD, 7% will be charged, thus 2,100 USD support.

In this simulation, the total value of Support Mission Critical will be $ 8,100 in the month.
