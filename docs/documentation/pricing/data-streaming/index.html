On Demand Pricing

The product usage will be billed, by sum of the values ​​calculated for two metrics: Data Streaming Requests and Data Streamed.

Data Streaming Requests

This is the total number of events sent by the Data Streaming product to endpoints, calculated as below, for every 10,000 requests:

Data Streaming Requests (per 10,000) Pricing
First 1 billion requests / month $0.024
Next 4 billion requests / month $0.010
Next 15 billion requests / month $0.004
Over 15 billion requests / month $0.002

Data Streamed

This is the total volume of data trafficked through the Data Streaming product, calculated as below, per GB:

Data Streamed (per GB) Pricing
First 10 TB / month $0.085
Next 40 TB / month $0.080
Next 100 TB / month $0.060
Next 350 TB / month $0.040
Next 500 TB / month $0.030
Next 4 PB / month $0.025
Over 5 PB / month $0.020

Pricing Example

If your app generated 3 billion events sent by Data Streaming in 1 month, consuming 25 TB of data, the value will be calculated as follows:

Data Streaming Requests

The first 1 billion requests is calculated at the price of 0.024 USD for every 10,000 requests, so (1 billion / 10,000) x 0.024 USD = 2400 USD.

The next 2 billion requests are priced at 0.010 USD for every 10,000 requests, so (2 billion / 10,000) x 0.010 USD = 2,000 USD.

In this example, the value for the month will be 4,400 USD.

Data Streamed

The first 10 TB will be priced at $ 0.085 per GB, so $ 10,000 x $ 0.085 = $ 850.

The next 15 TB will be priced at $ 0.080 per GB, so 15,000 GB x $ 0.080 = $ 1,200.

As an example, the value in the month will be 2,050 USD.

Amount

The total amount to be paid in the month will be 6,450 USD, according to the simulation.
