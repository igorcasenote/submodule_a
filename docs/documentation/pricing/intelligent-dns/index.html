On Demand Pricing

You will be charged depending on the use of Intelligent DNS, for the sum of the values ​​calculated for the metrics Hosted Zones and Standard Queries:

Hosted Zones

You will be charged for the total number of hosted zones at Azion.

Your amount of hosted zones may vary throughout the month. You will be charged for the average amount of zones over 12 hours.

Hosted Zones Price per hosted zone
First 25 25.00 USD
26 to 10.000 15.00 USD
Over 10.000 10.00 USD

Standard Queries

It is the total volume of DNS queries.

Standard Queries Price per million queries
First billion queries / month 0.85 USD
Over 1 billion queries / month 0.40 USD

Pricing Example

If you have hosted 30 zones in Intelligent DNS and received 1.5 billion queries over the month, the value will be calculated as follows:

Hosted Zones

The first 25 zones are priced at a unit price of 25 USD, thus 625 USD.

The remaining 5 zones are priced at a unit price of 15 USD, hence 75 USD.

In this example, the 30 zones will cost 700 USD.

Standard Queries

The first billion queries will be priced at $ 0.85 per million. Therefore (1,000,000,000 / 1,000,000) x 0.85 USD = 850 USD.

The remaining 500 million will be priced at $ 0.40 USD per million. So 500 x 0.40 USD = 200 USD.

In this example, the calculated value will be 1,050 USD.

Amount

The total amount to be paid in the month will be 1,750 USD, according to the simulation.
