Pricing Example

If your applications have 300 TB traffic in a month, much of that volume is data, such as payload, and some metadata, such as HTTP headers, the request made, the status, or user information.

Real-Time Events collects some of the metadata, which is available for your query. The metadata collected represents on average 0.2% of your traffic.

In this simulation, 600 GB (300 TB x 0.2%) will be collected.

The first 400 GB will be billed at a unit price of 1.50, ie 400 x 1.50 = 600.

The 200 GB remaining will be billed at a unit price of 1.20, ie 200 x 1.20 = 240.

The total calculated amount in this simulation will be 840 USD, considering the 1,000 queries were not exceeded.
